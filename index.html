A Royal Pursuit

Corner the Queen

Two players, anywhere in the world. One queen. Whoever herds her into the corner wins.

Starting zone Corner (target) Legal move

How to Play

  1. Create a game and send the link to a friend, or open a link a friend sent you.
  2. Once connected, the host places the queen anywhere in the glowing top row or right column.
  3. Players then alternate moving her any distance left, down, or diagonally down-left.
  4. Whoever moves the queen onto the gold corner wins.
