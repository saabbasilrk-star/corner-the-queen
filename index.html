Corner the Queen

Two players. One queen. Whoever herds her into the corner wins.

How to Play

  1. The board is 16 × 16.
  2. Player 1 places the queen anywhere in the glowing top row or right column.
  3. Players then alternate moving her any distance left, down, or diagonally down-left.
  4. Whoever moves the queen onto the gold corner wins.
